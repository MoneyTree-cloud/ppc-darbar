About Elan The Statement Gurgaon

Elan The Statement is a luxury residential project by Elan Group in Sector 49, Sohna Road, Gurgaon. The project offers spacious 4 BHK apartments (~4,300 sq ft) and penthouses (~5,500 sq ft) across 5 high-rise towers rising to G+38 floors.

Each floor has only 2 apartments, ensuring maximum privacy and low-density living. The 6-acre campus features 80% open space, a 1,00,000 sq ft clubhouse, grand double-height entrance lobbies, and VRV air-conditioning in all major rooms.

Designed by globally renowned Benoy Architects and constructed by Leighton India, Elan The Statement delivers an unmatched blend of architectural excellence, premium finishes, and world-class amenities for discerning homebuyers.

Read More About Elan The Statement Sector 49

The project is located on Sohna Road, one of Gurgaon's most strategic corridors, offering seamless connectivity to NH-48, Southern Peripheral Road, Golf Course Extension Road, IGI Airport, and Cyber City. The neighborhood includes top schools like GD Goenka and DPS, premium hospitals such as Medanta and Artemis, and luxury shopping and dining destinations.

Elan Group (Elan Limited) is one of North India's most trusted real estate developers, known for delivering landmark residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects with superior quality and timely delivery. Elan The Statement is RERA registered (GGM/1022/754/2025/125) with expected possession by October 2029.

Residents will enjoy amenities including a swimming pool, fitness arena, yoga and meditation decks, children's activity zones, jogging and cycling tracks, indoor sports courts (squash, billiards), mini theatre, skating rink, golf putting greens, landscaped gardens, and 24x7 smart-access gated security. The 30:40:30 payment plan makes it accessible for both end-users and investors looking at luxury apartments in Sector 49, Gurgaon.
