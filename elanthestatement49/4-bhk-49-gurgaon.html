Elan The Statement Sector 49 Gurgaon - Luxury 4 BHK Apartments on Sohna Road

Elan The Statement presents an exclusive collection of luxury 4 BHK residences crafted for families who desire spacious living, refined interiors, and a premium lifestyle in the heart of Gurgaon. Designed with a focus on comfort and elegance, these homes offer expansive layouts, smart spatial planning, and floor-to-ceiling windows that bring in abundant natural light.

Situated in Sector 49 on Sohna Road Road, one of Gurgaon’s most prestigious residential corridors, the project enjoys seamless connectivity to corporate business districts, IGI Airport, NH-48, and the proposed metro network. Residents benefit from proximity to top-tier schools, reputed hospitals, premium shopping malls, and vibrant entertainment hubs — making daily life convenient and enriching.

Each apartment is thoughtfully curated to deliver a luxurious living experience with wide balconies, premium fittings, designer kitchens, and spacious bedrooms. The residences are complemented by access to a 70,000 sq.ft. world-class clubhouse, featuring a resort-style swimming pool, advanced fitness center, indoor sports arena, wellness spaces, and multiple lifestyle amenities that elevate everyday living.

Whether you're upgrading to a bigger home, looking for privacy, or seeking a long-term luxury investment, the 4 BHK apartments at Elan The Statement offer a perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and high-value appreciation potential.
